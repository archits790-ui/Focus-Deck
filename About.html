About Focus-Deck

What is Focus-Deck?

Focus-Deck is your all-in-one productivity dashboard Created By Archit Singh, designed to help you organize your tasks, manage your studies, and maintain focus. It combines several powerful tools into a single, clean interface, storing all your data locally in your browser for 100% privacy.

Core Features

  • Focus Timer & Ambient Sounds: A built-in Pomodoro timer with 'Focus', 'Short Break', and 'Long Break' modes. Now includes an Ambient Sound Mixer (Rain, Forest, Cafe) to block out distractions.
  • Streak Tracking: Stay motivated by building a daily focus streak. The app tracks your current and longest streaks and rewards you with badges.
  • To-Do List with Categories: A powerful task manager that supports subtasks, recurring tasks, and a completed history. You can now organize tasks with Custom Categories (e.g., 'Urgent', 'Casual').
  • Flashcards: Create custom flashcard decks to study definitions or concepts. Test yourself with a simple flip-card interface.
  • Daily Journal: A dedicated space to log your daily thoughts, reflections, or achievements in a dated format.
  • Syllabus Manager: Organize your study subjects and topics. Track your progress for each subject with a visual percentage circle.
  • Weekly Timetable: Plan your week with a color-coded timetable. Mark activities as completed as you go.
  • Analytics: Visualize your productivity with charts for your syllabus progress, task completion, and timetable adherence.
  • Sticky Notes: A virtual canvas to jot down quick notes, ideas, or reminders. Notes are draggable and support attached links.
  • History Calendar: Look back at your accomplishments on a simple calendar view, which shows days you completed tasks, syllabus topics, or journal entries.
  • Workspaces: Separate your life into different contexts (e.g., 'Work', 'School', 'Personal') with independent data for each.
  • Advanced Customization: Switch between light and dark themes, pick accent colors, and even customize specific button colors in the settings.
